Solo Women Traveler Tips

  • Accommodation: Choose well-reviewed hotels or homestays with CCTV and 24/7 reception.
  • Travel Timing: Avoid traveling alone late at night, especially in isolated areas or using unverified transport.
  • Local Communication: Learn a few basic Kannada phrases (like 'Yellidira?' - Where is it?) to show confidence.
  • Share Location: Always share your real-time GPS location with a trusted contact back home.
  • Emergency App: Download and keep emergency contact numbers and the local Police/Women Helpline ready.

Family & Children Safety

  • Water Safety: Near waterfalls or beaches, always supervise children closely. Water currents can be deceptive.
  • ID Tags: Ensure children wear an ID tag with the hotel name and your contact number, especially in crowded tourist spots like Mysore Palace or Hampi.
  • Trekking Gear: For treks, ensure children have proper footwear and stick to marked trails with the family.
  • Snacks & Hydration: Carry sufficient water and pre-packaged snacks to avoid hygiene issues in remote areas.
  • Stranger Awareness: Teach children the 'No-Go-Yell' rule if approached by strangers.
