Kumara Parvatha,Near Subramanya

One of the most challenging treks in Karnataka, known for its dense forests and steep climbs. Reward: stunning views of the Western Ghats.

Difficulty: Hard | Duration: 2 days (30km total)

Mullayanagiri Peak

The highest peak in Karnataka, located in the Chikmagalur region. The trek is moderate and the summit offers panoramic misty views.

Difficulty: Moderate | Duration: 3 hours

Kodachadri, near Shivamogga

A natural heritage site, popular for its unique sunset view over the Arabian Sea. It involves trekking through dense jungle and grasslands.

Difficulty: Moderate/Hard | Duration: 1 day

Bandaje Falls Trek, near Kudremukha

Popular for its Stunning Waterfall and dense forest trails, offering a thrilling experience for adventure enthusiasts.

Difficulty: Hard | Duration: 1 day

Ettina Bhuja Trek, near Sakleshpura

Popular for its Panaromic Views of Western Ghats and Bull Shoulder like Rock Structure/p>

Difficulty: Easy | Duration: Half day

Trekking Safety Tip:

Always trek with a registered guide and ensure you carry adequate water and first aid, especially in isolated sections of the Western Ghats.
