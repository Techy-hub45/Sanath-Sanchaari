üåä Majestic Waterfalls of Karnataka

1. Jog Falls, Shivamogga

The second highest plunge waterfall in India, formed by the Sharavathi River. It separates into four distinct cascades: Raja, Rani, Roarer, and Rocket. The view from the viewpoint is spectacular, especially during the monsoon season.

Best Time to Visit: August to December.

2. Abbey Falls, Coorg

Located near Madikeri, this beautiful waterfall is nestled among private coffee plantations and spice estates. The hanging bridge offers a picturesque view of the cascading water.

Note: Easily accessible, but requires a short walk through the estates.

3. Barachukki Falls, Shivanasamudra

A major tourist attraction at shivanasamudra near Mandya,formed by Kaveri River.It is a segmented waterfall and is one of the two waterfalls that make up the Shivanasamudra Falls,The other being Gaganachukki Falls

Accessibility: Vehicle accessible.


4. Mallalli Falls,Sakleshpura

A Stunning Waterfall located in Bisile Reserve Forest,formed by kumaradhara river sorrounding by lush greenery that offers unimaginable scenery

Accessibility: Vehicle accessible


5. Unchalli Falls, Sirsi

Also Known as Lushington Falls,formed by Aghanashini River,with a height of 116 meters.The Waterfall is sorrounded by thick forest making it a perfect spot for nature lovers.

Accessibility: short trek from a main road


6. Hebbe Falls, Chikmagalur

A beautiful waterfall located in the Chikmagalur district, known for its two-tiered cascade and surrounded by coffee plantations and dense forests.

Accessibility:local Jeeps Available at starting point or you can trek towords falls also
